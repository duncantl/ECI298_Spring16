The Data Science Initiative on campus is an effort to

  1. Support and evangelize Data-Driven Research across all disciplines

  2. Train researchers (faculty, staff, graduate students) and undergraduates in data science

  3. Promote multidisciplinary, team-based research

What is Data Science ? Very broad in 2 ways.

  1. Affects all disciplines - from Environmental and Earth Sciences, to Physics, Biology, to Economics & Political Science, to English, History, etc.

  2. Involves the entire data pipeline from

    • Framing a question(s)

    • Identifying data (found data on the Web, experimental design)

    • Collecting/Accessing data

    • Managing and structuring data

    • Validating, cleaning and transforming data

    • Exploratory Data Analysis, Visualization

    • Statistical and Machine learning for modeling, testing, inference

    • Interpreation of resulrs

    • Conveying the insights (e.g., dashboards)

    • Data governance, curation, privacy, ethics, ...

This is a strong mix of

  • Domain knowledge of the right questions and interpretation

  • Data Analysis

  • Data Technologies

  • Computing

  • Exploration and Visualization

  • Skepticism of the data and how they were collected and what they represent

What can the DSI do for you? We'd like to hear what you want.

  1. Seminars, tutorials, coffee hours we organize next year, and during the summer

  2. Consulting on any Data Science aspect of your research (office hours)

  3. Collaboration on bigger projects that need the combination of your and our multidisciplinary skills (our call for projects).

  4. Become affiliates and hang out in the DSI space and create a multidisciplinary community, in addition to your home program. https://docs.google.com/forms/d/18Tj5jMgueVm2eqfgxKh1AWxsNi3ZgoS-x7WribUzFj8/viewform

  5. Workshops - suggest some, come to ones we organize
